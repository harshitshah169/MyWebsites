Mountain bike

A mountain bicycle is a bicycle with a light sturdy frame, mostly short heighted, broad deep-treaded tyres, and multiple gears, originally designed for riding on mountainous terrain.

Price: 3000/- onwards

Road Bike

Road bikes are bicycles designed to take you as far and as fast as your legs can manage. The road bike gets its name from the terrain it is designed to be used on – the road.

Price: 3000/- onwards

Hybrid Bike

A hybrid is exactly what it sounds like – a mix of a road bike and a mountain bike. The result is a bicycle that’s suitable for general-purpose riding over various types of terrain.

Price: 3000/- onwards
